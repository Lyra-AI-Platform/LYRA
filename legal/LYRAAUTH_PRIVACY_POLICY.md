## What We Collect

When you solve a LyraAuth challenge:

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Your challenge answer | AI training data | Indefinitely (anonymized) |
| Time taken to answer | Bot detection | 30 days, then deleted |
| Browser user agent | Bot detection | 30 days, then deleted |

**We do NOT collect:** name, email, IP address (in training data), cookies, location,
browsing history, or any other personally identifiable information tied to your answers.

---

## How We Use It

Your answer becomes an anonymized training example:
```
{"prompt": "The sky is ___", "completion": "blue"}
```

This record contains NO information about you personally. It is used solely to
improve the Lyra AI language model's understanding of natural language.

## Data Sharing

We may share anonymized, aggregated training data with:
- Academic researchers studying AI language models
- Open-source AI projects (under CC-BY license)

We will NEVER sell individual-level data or share it with advertising networks.

---

## Cookies

LyraAuth uses a session cookie (lyraauth_session) to prevent replay attacks.
This cookie is:
- Session-only (deleted when browser closes)
- Contains only a random session identifier
- Not used for tracking or advertising

---

## Children's Privacy

LyraAuth is not directed at children under 13. Website operators who serve
children must obtain verifiable parental consent before deploying LyraAuth.

---

## Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Challenge answers are stored in encrypted
files accessible only to authorized systems.
